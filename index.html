Duke Dash Posted On: Saturday, August 25, 2018

Join us for a fun autumn run through Essexville at the Inaugural Duke Dash!  In addition to the race, there will be events for the kids such as pumpkin decorating, games, face painting and more!  Our brand new concessions trailer will also be on site with food to purchase as well as apple cider slushies!  We are so excited to demonstrate our commitment to health and wellness and an active lifestyle.  All proceeds will go toward equipment to improve our schools' playgrounds.  

 

Go to www.e-hps.net/dukedash for more information or click here for more information.
